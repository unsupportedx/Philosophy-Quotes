Philosophy is a single-page web application that presents quotes from renowned philosophers on existence, pain, tragedy, and life through a cinematic experience.
Against a pure AMOLED black background, quotes emerge slowly from darkness — inspired by the death screen in Call of Duty. The app opens with a language selector offering English, Turkish, and Spanish, with over 200 quotes across all three languages. Quotes are shuffled randomly each session and advance automatically between 5 and 15 seconds depending on their length; users can also tap the screen to skip ahead. For the first 10 seconds, control buttons are visible for navigating previous and next quotes, taking a screenshot, and toggling fullscreen — after which the interface fades away, leaving only the quote. Keyboard shortcuts are also supported.
